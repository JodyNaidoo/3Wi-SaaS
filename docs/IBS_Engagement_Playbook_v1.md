## The strategic insight

| Two truths that live together |
|---|
| **Brand is the asset** → Jody cannot be replaced as the front (sales, judgment, relationship) |
| **Delivery is the cost** → Phases 2-4 CAN be productised at the back (execution, reporting, coordination) |

Strategy: **Keep Jody at the front. Standardise the back. Insert a "Senior Coordinator" role as the bridge.** Same model as top consultancies (Partners sell + judge, Associates execute).

## Pricing — 3 strategic tiers

### Tier A — Strategic Partner
- **When:** Stepping-stone client, multi-year horizon, market leader
- **Structure:** R 40-80k/month retainer + outcome share + 12-month min
- **Jody intensity:** 8-12 hrs/month
- **Example:** Hempire ECRDA programme

### Tier B — Fixed Engagement
- **When:** Clear-scope project, defined deliverables, budget set
- **Structure:** R 150-450k total · 30/40/30 milestone draws · 3-6 months
- **Jody intensity:** 8-15 hrs front-loaded + 2-4 hrs at milestones
- **Example:** Specific grant application, market entry study, HR restructure

### Tier C — Tactical Sprint
- **When:** Foot-in-door, cash-flow accelerator, "look-see" before commitment
- **Structure:** R 25-75k · 2-4 weeks · Productised deliverable
- **Jody intensity:** 2-4 hrs total
- **Example:** Market scan, strategy review, grant readiness assessment
